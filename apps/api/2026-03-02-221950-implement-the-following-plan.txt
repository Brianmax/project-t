  The consumption side works correctly on its own. Here's how
  calculateConsumptionForPeriod works:

  1. It looks for a reading tagged billingMonth=X, billingYear=Y вАФ the current
  reading
  2. It finds the most recent reading from any prior month вАФ the previous
  reading
  3. Consumption = currentReading - previousReading

  So for a tenant leaving on Feb 15:
  - Staff physically reads the meter on Feb 15 and enters it into the system
  tagged as billingMonth=2, billingYear=2026
  - Receipt generation finds that reading as the "current" reading for February
  - Subtracts the January reading вЖТ correct consumption for the days occupied

  The proration and consumption are independent by design, which is correct вАФ
  rent is day-based, but consumption is meter-based. You bill exactly what the
  meter shows was consumed.
